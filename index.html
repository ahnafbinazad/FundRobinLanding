Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FundRobin.ai and our early access program.

When will FundRobin launch?

FundRobin is scheduled to launch in Q3 2025. Early access members will receive invitations to our beta program starting in late summer.

How does the AI grant matching work?

Our AI analyzes your organization's profile, mission, and previous work to identify grants with the highest likelihood of approval. We consider eligibility criteria, past award patterns, and alignment with funders' priorities to suggest the best opportunities.

Who can sign up for early access?

Early access is open to nonprofit organizations, NGOs, educational institutions, and other entities that regularly apply for grants. We're particularly interested in working with diverse organizations across different sectors and sizes.

Will my data be secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we follow industry best practices for data protection. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security for all user information.
